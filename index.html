The Problem

U.S. dog owners use approximately 27 billion plastic bags every year. They end up in landfills, waterways, and oceans — and they don’t biodegrade. Biodegradable alternatives don’t either, not in the anaerobic conditions of a landfill. The Poddy eliminates the bag entirely.

How It Works

Three steps.
No plastic. No smell.

1

Scoop

The built-in claw retrieves waste cleanly — no hand contact, no thin bag between you and the problem.

2

Seal

The odor-proof lid locks everything inside. Carry it home clipped to your belt, leash, or bag. Nobody knows it’s there.

3

Empty

Tip the contents into the toilet — the EPA-recommended method. Rinse. Done. Ready for the next walk.

The Impact

Small action.
Massive scale.

One Poddy replaces 365 bags per year. At 1% adoption across U.S. dog owners, 657 million bags are eliminated annually. Dog waste properly routed to treatment plants could generate over $1.4 billion in biogas and fertilizer value — currently going entirely to landfill.
