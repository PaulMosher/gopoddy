The Problem We're Solving

The numbers are
staggering.

Every year, U.S. dog owners use 27 billion plastic waste bags. These bags sit in landfills for hundreds of years, releasing microplastics and methane as they slowly degrade.

Even "biodegradable" bags mostly end up in landfills where conditions prevent proper decomposition. Well-meaning pet owners are paying extra for a product that still harms the planet.

The Poddy changes that — one walk at a time.

Our Story

8 years in
the making.

First hand-drawn concept sketch for a reusable, odor-proof dog waste tool.
May 2020
Founded Mosher Group, LLC. Taught ourselves 3D design with iPad Pros and Apple Pencils. Purchased a 3D printer and built our first prototype.

Multiple prototype iterations. Every feature tested in real-world walks with Teddy, our loyal test dog.

Now
Preparing for our prototype launch. Join the waitlist to be first in line.

At Go Poddy, we believe responsible pet ownership should be simple, enjoyable, and kind to the planet. What started as a sketch on December 27, 2019 grew into years of research, design, prototyping, and real-world testing.

After confirming what we already suspected — that the dog waste bag industry produces enough plastic to fill 5,440 Olympic swimming pools every year — we knew there had to be a better way.

We taught ourselves 3D design from scratch, invested in a 3D printer, and tested every prototype with our loyal test dog, Teddy. Key innovations like the built-in scooping claw, flared container opening, and odor-proof seal set the Poddy apart from anything else on the market.

Today, Go Poddy is more than an invention — it's a call to action for dog owners who want to do right by their pets, their neighbors, and the planet.
